Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun and the smallest planet in the solar system.

Venus

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in structure to Earth, but with a thick, toxic atmosphere.

Earth

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet and is home to the tallest volcano and the deepest, longest canyon in the solar system.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system and is known for its Great Red Spot, a giant storm.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning rings, made up of ice and rock particles.

Uranus

Uranus is an ice giant with a unique tilt that makes it appear to roll along its orbit.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and is known for its deep blue color and strong winds.
